BENEBOX BY CSOCIETY

BeneBox is a Multi-modal Therapeutic Box to holistically improve mental health, access to basic resources and combatting lack of interaction for vulnerable members in society.

What is BeneBox?

BeneBox is a system that lets you provide gifts for your loved ones, even if the COVID-19 pandemic has separated you from them. You simply purchase a box, and a team of dedicated employees and volunteers will handle its procurement and delivery. You can even add a personal letter to tell your loved ones that even though you may be physically separated, you're not socially distant.

Our Story

The Hacker Exchange Great Global Challenge: A Five-Day Ideas-Hack is what brought us students from Monash University, Australian National University and University of California, San Diego to create the BeneBox to help the community that is affected by the COVID-19 pandemic.
