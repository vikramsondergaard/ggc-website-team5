BENEBOX BY CSOCIETY

What is BeneBox?

BeneBox is a Multi-modal Therapeutic Box to holistically improve mental health, access to basic resources and combatting lack of interaction for vulnerable members in society.

Even if the COVID-19 pandemic has separated you from your loved ones, BeneBox can keep you together. You simply purchase a box, and a team of dedicated employees and volunteers will handle its procurement and delivery. You can even add a personal letter to tell your loved ones that even though you may be physically separated, you're not socially distant.

Who is BeneBox for?

cSociety's BeneBox is for everyone who is struggling to find a connection and looking to holistically increase their mental health during isolation caused by the pandemic.

Though we are focusing on the elderly population in nursing and assisted living homes who are in isolation to make sure that they are kept connected inside their homes.

Our Story

The Hacker Exchange Great Global Challenge: A Five-Day Ideas-Hack is what brought us students from Monash University, Australian National University and University of California, San Diego to create the BeneBox to help the community that is affected by the COVID-19 pandemic.
